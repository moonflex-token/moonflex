Liquidity Incentive

MoonFlex incentivizes customers to provide liquidity. To kick-start our liquidity pool, we are offering bonus tokens for liquidity providers. Here’s how it works:

  • 📈 Bonus to liquidity providers until we reach $500,000 in our liquidity pool.
  • 💰 Minimum liquidity of $10,000 qualifies for a bonus:
    • 10,000$ or more: Bonus 1%
    • 20,000$ or more: Bonus 2.5%
    • 30,000$ or more: Bonus 4%
    • 50,000$ or more: Bonus 8%

    Bonus tokens will be released in tranches: 10% of the bonus every month over a 10-month period. Liquidity providers can sell tokens anytime, including the current month’s bonus. The bonuses are automatically sent to the address that provided the liquidity. If you want to confirm your participation, you can email us for bonus planning details.

    Note: Once our liquidity pool reaches $500,000, a different (less juicy) bonus structure will apply.
