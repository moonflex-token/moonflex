Our Roadmap

Our journey consists of three significant phases designed to ensure the successful establishment of the MoonFlex bill swap exchange:

  1. Phase 1: Establishing the Business

    We aim to raise $30,000 to kickstart our business operations. This phase includes:

    • Formation of LLC: We will establish a Limited Liability Company (LLC) to ensure legal compliance.
    • Licensing: Obtaining necessary licenses to operate a financial service in Cambodia.
    • Office Space: Renting an office in a high-visibility location to attract customers and partners.

  2. Phase 2: Setting Up Operations

    In this phase, we will invest in essential equipment to ensure a secure and efficient bill swapping process:

    • Testing Machines: We will procure advanced machines capable of verifying the authenticity of bills with multiple security features.
    • Security Systems: Installing a comprehensive camera security system to safeguard our operations.
    • Staff Training: Ensuring our team is well-trained in customer service and operational procedures.

  3. Phase 3: Marketing and Launch

    The final phase involves a robust marketing strategy to promote our services:

    • Digital Marketing Campaigns: Leveraging social media and online advertising to reach potential customers.
    • Community Engagement: Organizing local events and partnerships to build brand awareness.
    • Official Launch: Hosting a launch event to introduce our services to the public and generate excitement.

Our mission is to create a trustworthy platform that meets the needs of a growing market, enhancing the currency exchange experience for everyone.
